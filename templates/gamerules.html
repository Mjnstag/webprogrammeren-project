Super Trivia Explained


Welcome to Super Trivia! The goal of this game is to test your knowledge and compete with your friends. You start the game with a choice of categories. You have six different themes and three levels of difficulty to choose from. After that, you can choose your username.


Classic Mode: Once you have entered your username, you can start the game right away. No need to wait for your friends. You will receive ten questions. For every correct answer you will receive a point. If you score enough points, you'll be awarded a spot in the top 10!
Can you make it in there?

Custom Mode: In this game mode you can pick the amount of questions you want to play! What's cool about this game mode is that each amount of questions has their own high score. If you're feeling lucky, pick a high amount of questions and go for the top spot!
